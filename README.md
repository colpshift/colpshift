### About

I build enterprise integrations and automation on **ServiceNow** and **Angular** — flows, subflows, business rules, client scripts, and service portals that people actually rely on.

On the AI side, I work with **MCP (Model Context Protocol)**, **A2A**, **RAG pipelines**, and **Generative AI** — not as experiments, but as parts of a real engineering workflow.

My daily environment is **CachyOS Linux + Fish shell + VS Code**, with **Claude Code** running alongside every significant task. The IDE is a tool; the AI is a multiplier.

The goal is always the same: close the gap between what I know and what the problem needs.
